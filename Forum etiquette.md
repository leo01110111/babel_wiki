# Forum etiquette

## Forum Etiquette for Users and Contributors

### Purpose of Forum Etiquette

Forum etiquette refers to the code of conduct and guidelines that should be followed by users and contributors on a forum. It ensures that discussions remain productive, respectful, and inclusive for all participants. This wiki page provides an overview of forum etiquette for users and contributors in a MediaWiki instance.

### Guidelines for Users and Contributors

#### Be Respectful and Courteous

Respect is paramount when engaging in forum discussions. Treat others with kindness, even if you disagree with their viewpoints. Avoid personal attacks.

#### Use Clear and Concise Language

When posting on the forum, use clear and concise language to express your thoughts. Avoid using excessive jargon or complicated language that may confuse other participants.

#### Stay On Topic

Keep the discussions focused on the forum's theme or topic. Avoid introducing unrelated or off-topic discussions that may distract from the main purpose of the forum.

#### Search Before Posting

Before creating a new thread or asking a question, search the forum to see if a similar topic already exists. This helps avoid duplicate discussions and allows you to contribute to existing threads if applicable.

#### Provide Relevant Information

When asking a question or seeking assistance, provide as much relevant information as possible. This includes describing your issue or inquiry clearly and providing any relevant error messages, steps taken, and software versions.

#### Be Patient and Considerate

Remember that forums are often comprised of volunteers and community members who offer their help in their free time. Be patient while awaiting responses, and express gratitude for any assistance received.

#### Follow Forum Rules and Guidelines

Each forum may have its own set of rules and guidelines. Familiarize yourself with these rules and adhere to them while participating in discussions. This ensures a consistent and harmonious forum environment.

#### Respect Copyright and Intellectual Property

When sharing content or information on the forum, respect copyright laws and intellectual property rights. Do not post copyrighted material without proper permission or attribution.

#### Report Violations

If you encounter any behavior that violates the forum's guidelines or code of conduct, report it to the forum moderators or administrators. This helps maintain a healthy and respectful forum community.

#### Contribute Constructively

Whether you are a user seeking help or a contributor offering assistance, strive to contribute constructively to the discussions. Provide insightful and helpful responses, offer guidance, and encourage collaboration among participants.

Remember, forum etiquette is essential for creating a welcoming and productive environment for all users and contributors. By following these guidelines, you can actively contribute to the forum community and make it a valuable resource for everyone involved.
